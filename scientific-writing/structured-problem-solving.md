# Structured Problem-Solving Framework: Clinical Operations Case Study
**Author:** [Amit Shrishrimal]  
**Methodology Source:** McKinsey Forward Principles & Scientific Writing Frameworks

This case study demonstrates the application of structured management frameworks and clear scientific exposition to solve operational bottlenecks within clinical and pharmaceutical environments.

## 1. Problem Definition (The SMART Framework)
*   **Specific:** Clinical trial site recruitment rates are falling below targets for a Phase III trial.
*   **Measurable:** Current enrolment is at 45% of the quarterly target milestone.
*   **Achievable:** Historical benchmarks show identical sites can reach 90% within normal timelines.
*   **Relevant:** Delays directly impact regulatory submission timelines and burn venture capital.
*   **Time-bound:** Resolution required within the next 45 business days.
